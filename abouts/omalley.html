O'Malley

Abraham DeLacey Giuseppe Casey Thomas O'Malley was a smooth-talking, streetwise cat who relishes his life of freedom out in the open with no rules and no responsibilities. One day he met a beautiful white fured lady named Duchess, a fellow singer and musician. The two eventually married and he is not the proud step-father of three musically gifted children!
